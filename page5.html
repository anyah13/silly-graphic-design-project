LIMINAL SPACES


do you ever see a photo that feels familiar

like you've been there before,

but something's just a little bit off?

---

these photos show old, abandoned, or broken down places

the locations are usually malls, interior of houses, office buildings, etc.

the main thing every liminal space has is its emptiness.

the lack of people in a place the would otherwise be populated creates an uncomfortable feeling.

---

for me, something about these photos brings me more comfort than discomfort.

i find the stillness of these places peaceful

its somewhere that i would love to walk around and exist in for a little while.




but there still is that twinge of existential dread in the back of my head

whenever i see those empty, poorly lit, stagnant places that seem to forverver be stuck in this eerie state of in-between.
